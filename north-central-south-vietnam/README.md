## How the destination was chosen
Filtered the explorer-historian shortlist (Vietnam, Taiwan, Sri Lanka, Japan, Georgia, Uzbekistan, Oman) against four constraints:

1. **Middle East routing** — avoid any itinerary connecting through Gulf hubs (Dubai/Emirates, Doha/Qatar, Abu Dhabi/Etihad) or overflying the conflict zone.
2. **Late-Nov/early-Dec weather.**
3. **Group of 8–10 + ~10k PLN/person.**
4. **Profile fit** — history + nature + unusual transport + viewpoints.

**Vietnam won** on all four at once: densest profile match, most forgiving budget, dry-season weather (via north-first sequencing), and Gulf-free routing. Taiwan was runner-up; Japan strained the budget; **Oman was excluded** despite a perfect season because it sits on the Strait of Hormuz inside the flagged risk zone; Georgia/Uzbekistan are off-season (cold, short days) in this window.

## Assumptions (confirmed)
- **Assembly + departure hub:** **Warsaw (WAW)** — whole group flies together on one ticket. Members in Gliwice (~3 h rail) and Zielona Góra (~4.5 h rail / feeder flight) travel to Warsaw the day before.
- **Dates:** **Fri 27 Nov → Tue 15 Dec 2026**, ~17 days on the ground (extended from 14 — see itinerary for why). Leave needed: ~12 working days.
- **Budget scope:** 10,000 PLN per person covers **international flights + accommodation**. In-country food, internal transport, tours and the DAD→SGN flight are additional (still comfortably affordable in Vietnam — see budget file).
- **Group profile:** itinerary is built to your personal explorer-historian style; if the wider group skews toward slower/comfort travel, the pace can be eased.

## Flight routing (locked) — Warsaw, Turkish Airlines, open-jaw via Istanbul
The whole group flies together from Warsaw on **one Turkish Airlines ticket**, connecting at Istanbul (IST) — the cleanest Middle-East-free routing (Turkish routes **north of the closed airspace**; no Gulf hub).

- **Out:** WAW → **Istanbul (IST)** → **Hanoi (HAN)**.
- **Back:** **Saigon (SGN)** → **Istanbul (IST)** → WAW (open-jaw, no backtracking).
- **Indicative fare:** ~€550–800 ≈ 2,400–3,500 PLN return, well inside the flights+hotels budget.

**Route check (verified against 2026-schedule airline data, Nov 2026):**
- Turkish Airlines operates WAW⇄IST, IST⇄Hanoi (Noi Bai) and IST⇄Saigon (Tan Son Nhat). ✓
- No nonstop exists from any Polish airport to Vietnam (LOT's Asian long-haul is Bangkok/Seoul/Tokyo/Beijing). ✓
- **Backup** if Turkish fares spike: Vietnam Airlines nonstop into Frankfurt/Paris/London + a short feeder to Warsaw.

- **Avoid:** Emirates, Qatar Airways, Etihad — the Gulf hubs are the chokepoint, closing at short notice in the current situation.
- **Still to confirm at booking:** exact flight numbers, times and live fares (a booking engine is needed — search was blocked in this session).

## ⚠️ Caveat — verify before booking
The airspace picture is **fluid** and was assessed under limited search access in this environment. Re-check the current situation close to booking via official sources (EASA Conflict Zone Information Bulletins, NOTAMs, safeairspace.net) and confirm your chosen carrier's actual routing. Treat the routing guidance here as a starting filter, not a guarantee.
